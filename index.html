How to Play
If the digits on your tickets match the winning numbers in the correct order,you win a portion of the prize pool.
STEP 1
Buy Tickets
Prices are set when the round starts,equal to5 USD in CAKE per ticket.
STEP 2
Wait for the Draw
There is one draw every day alternating between O AM UTC and 12 PM UTC.
STEP 3
Check for Prizes
Once the round’s over,come back to the page and check to see if you’ve won!

Winning Criteria

The digits on your ticket must match in the orrect order to win.

Here’s an example lottery draw,with two tickets,A and B.

  • Ticket A:The first 3 digits and the last 2 digits match,but the 4th digit is wrong,so this ticket only wins a
  • Ticket B:Even though the last 5 digits match,the first digit is wrong, so this ticket doesn’t win a prize.

Proze brackets don’t’stack’:if you match the first 3 digits in order,you’ll only win prizes from the’Match 3’ bracket,and not from’Match 1’ and ‘Match 2’.

Winning Criteria

The prizes for each lottery round come from three sources:

Ticket Purchases

  • 100% of the CAKE paid by people buying tickets that round goes back into the prize pools.

Rollover Prizes

  • After every round,if nobody wins in one of the prize brackt,the unclaimed CAKEfor that bracket rolls over into the next round and are redisributed among the prize pools.

CAKE Injections

  • An average total of 35,000 CAKE from the treasury is added to lottery rounds over the course of a week. This CAKE is of course also included rollovers!Read more in our guide to CAKE Tokenomics
